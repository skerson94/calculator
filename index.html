How much do at-home COVID tests cost?

By Sarah Kerson

Consumers now have several options for at-home COVID-19 tests. The Biden administration has a website where Americans can order free at-home tests, but tests are limited to four per household and can take weeks to arrive. Insurance plans are required to cover the cost of at-home testing kits, but shoppers may still need to get reimbursed and pay an upfront cost.
